ABOUT REMI+

Revolutionising Healthcare Appointment Management

Remi+ is an AI-powered healthcare appointment assistant designed to simplify appointment booking, reduce administrative workload, and improve the overall patient experience through intelligent automation.

Why We Created Remi+

Healthcare providers spend a significant amount of time managing appointments, answering repetitive enquiries, and handling manual scheduling. These repetitive tasks increase administrative workload and reduce the time available for patient care.

Remi+ was developed to automate these routine processes using Artificial Intelligence. By combining conversational AI with workflow automation, Remi+ enables patients to book appointments, receive reminders, and obtain healthcare information anytime, while allowing clinics to manage appointments more efficiently.
