FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about Remi+.

What is Remi+?

Remi+ is an AI-powered healthcare appointment assistant that helps users manage appointments, answer healthcare-related questions, and improve scheduling efficiency.

How can I manage my appointments?

Visit the Appointment Dashboard to view, update, or delete your appointments in real time.

Can Remi+ create appointments?

Yes. New appointments can be created through the Appointment Booking page, while the AI assistant can help manage existing appointments.

How long does it take to receive a reply?

Our team typically responds within one business day.
